Maruti Suzuki Placement Preparation Guide

This section provides complete preparation guidance for Maruti Suzuki recruitment including eligibility criteria, placement syllabus, interview process, job roles and preparation strategy.

About Maruti Suzuki Company

Company Overview

  • Maruti Suzuki is India's largest automobile manufacturer.
  • Founded in 1981 as Maruti Udyog Limited.
  • Headquarters located in New Delhi, India.
  • Subsidiary of Suzuki Motor Corporation, Japan.
  • Produces passenger vehicles and automotive components.
  • Manufacturing plants located in Haryana and Gujarat.
  • Leader in Indian automobile market with large customer base.

Major Products

  • Passenger cars (Swift, Baleno, Alto, Dzire)
  • Hybrid vehicles
  • Automobile engines and components
  • Automotive research and development
  • Vehicle manufacturing and assembly
  • After-sales service and support

Eligibility Criteria

Academic Qualification

  • B.E / B.Tech (Mechanical, Electrical, E&TC, Production)
  • Minimum 60% throughout academics
  • No active backlogs
  • Strong technical fundamentals
  • Good communication skills

Required Skills

  • Mechanical and automobile fundamentals
  • Basic knowledge of manufacturing processes
  • Problem solving ability
  • Basic programming knowledge
  • Teamwork and communication skills
  • Knowledge of automation systems
