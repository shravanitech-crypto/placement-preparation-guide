Reading Comprehension Theory

Main Idea

Recognize what the passage is primarily about. Look for repeated themes and summary sentences.

Supporting Details

Pick out facts, examples, or reasons that back up the main idea.

Making Inferences

Read between the lines to understand ideas that are not directly stated.

Author’s Tone & Purpose

Identify the writer’s attitude and reason for writing the text.

Drawing Conclusions

Combine information from the text with your own reasoning to reach logical outcomes.
