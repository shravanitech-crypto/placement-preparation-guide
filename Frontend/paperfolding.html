Paper Folding questions test your spatial visualization ability. A paper is folded and patterns overlap. You must imagine the final result after folding.

📖 Paper Folding Theory

What is Paper Folding?

Paper folding problems involve folding a sheet and predicting how shapes overlap when folded.

Observation

Observe the original shape carefully. Identify lines, shapes and folding direction.

Visualization

Mentally imagine how the paper folds and how patterns overlap.

Elimination Method

Remove clearly incorrect options and choose the best matching folded pattern.
