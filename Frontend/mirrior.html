📖 Mirror Image Theory

What is Mirror Image?

A mirror image is the reflection of an object seen in a mirror. The left and right sides are reversed due to a concept called lateral inversion.

Lateral Inversion

Lateral inversion means the interchange of the left side and right side in a mirror. However, the top and bottom remain the same.

Symmetrical Letters

Some letters look the same even after mirror reflection.

  • A, H, I, M, O
  • T, U, V, W
  • X, Y

Important Tips

  • Left and right sides always interchange
  • Top and bottom remain same
  • Check first and last letters
  • Use clock shortcut: 11:60 – Time
